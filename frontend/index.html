Why NexGen
Everything under one roof.

From patient admission to discharge — every step is tracked, managed and accessible in real-time by the right people.

🔐
Secure Role-Based Access
Patients see their own data. Doctors see their assigned patients. Admins control everything. JWT-secured, BCrypt-hashed passwords.
🛏
Bed & Ward Management
Real-time bed occupancy tracking across 7 wards. Instant alerts when ICU or NICU capacity is critical.
📋
Digital Medical Records
Doctors create structured records — vitals, diagnosis, prescriptions, lab results — all stored securely in Neo4j graph database.
📅
Smart Appointment Scheduling
Admin schedules appointments, assigns doctors, sets dates and times. Patients receive instant visibility through their portal.
💊
Pharmacy & Billing
Track inventory levels, generate invoices, manage insurance claims — all from the Admin dashboard.
🔗
Neo4j Graph Backend
Patient–Doctor–Appointment relationships modelled as a graph for fast, flexible queries and analytics.
